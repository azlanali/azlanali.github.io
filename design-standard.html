02 — Logo mark

The mark

The Kaabah cube in isometric perspective, with the Masjid Nabawi umbrella canopy mandala projected onto the top face. Sacred geometry on sacred architecture — precision, heritage, and radial symmetry. The mark works in two colourways: black and gold (primary) and obsidian purple (secondary).

Symbol meaning

The Kaabah cube — perfect, absolute, symmetrical. The mandala on its roof is the umbrella canopy of Masjid Nabawi — radial, geometric, precise. Heritage and mathematics in one mark.

Logo rules

Never stretch or rotate the mark. Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter "A" around all sides. Never place on a busy background without sufficient contrast.

Gold for heritage contexts

Use black and gold when the context is formal, prestigious, or high-stakes. Presentations, personal website hero, print materials.

Purple for tech contexts

Use obsidian purple for engineering, AI, and digital product work. Dashboards, GitHub profile, technical writing.

Green for agrotech

Forest greens appear only in agricultural and environmental contexts. Never mixed with gold on the same surface.

One accent at a time

Never combine gold and purple on the same screen. Pick one per context and stay consistent throughout that surface.

04 — Typography

Type scale

Primary font: Geist Sans or Inter for all digital surfaces. Both are free and available on Google Fonts. Use a serif (Lora) only for long-form editorial content.

Display

40px · weight 500 · tracking -1px · hero titles only

Section heading

28px · weight 500 · tracking -0.5px · page sections

Subheading

18px · weight 500 · cards, labels, UI

Body text — where the real work lives. Clear, direct, and never padded. Good writing is precise: one idea per sentence, no filler.

15px · weight 400 · line-height 1.7 · all body copy

Caption / Label

11px · weight 500 · tracking 0.15em · uppercase · metadata, section labels

07 — Design rules

Principles

Clarity over decoration

No gradients, no heavy shadows. If an element carries no information, it shouldn't be there. Whitespace is the premium signal.

Complexity made simple

Every interface should look effortless. The hard work happens before the screen — not on it. This is the brand story in visual form.

Heritage as strength

The Islamic geometric references are not decoration. They are identity. Use them with intention, not as superficial ornament.

Gold anchors confidence

Gold (#C9A84C) is the primary brand colour. Use it for CTAs, key UI elements, and wherever you need to signal presence and authority.

Context switches colourway

Engineering and AI work uses obsidian purple. Formal and heritage contexts use black and gold. Agricultural work uses green. Never mix.

Sky is the limit

The mark is always reaching upward. Compositions should breathe — generous top space, never capped or crowded at the top of a frame.
